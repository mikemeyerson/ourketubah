     Elyse Stein Meyerson has been painting ketubot for five years. Formerly the senior graphic designer for the Central Park Zoo in New York, she studied at the world-renowned Bezalel Academy of Art and Design in Jerusalem and the Parsons School of Design in New York. She currently resides in Brooklyn New York with her husband and three children.

Couples can supple their own text or choose from a variety of religious and non-traditional texts from Orthodox, Conservative, Reform, Interfaith, and Commitment ceremonies.

These texts can be inscribed in Hebrew and English, Aramaic, or Hebrew alone.

It is recommended that the Rabbi or officiate at your ceremony approve whichever text you choose.
